\section*{}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Abstract}
\begin{abstract}
\begin{large}
    The present report addresses two key areas in the field of IT security and system administration. The first part presents an automated approach for analyzing and comparing Group Policy Objects (GPOs), enabling a systematic comparison between ABB’s internal configurations and those of customer environments. The goal is to efficiently identify deviations, security gaps, and opportunities for optimization. \\
    The second part focuses on the evaluation of modern obfuscation techniques used in penetration testing. Various methods are examined in terms of their effectiveness, detectability, and practical applicability. The combination of both topics provides a practical contribution to enhancing IT security in industrial environments.
\end{large}
\end{abstract}
